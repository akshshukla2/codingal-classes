About us!

South City International School, established in 2009, envisions the cultivation of young minds into truly global citizens within a nurturing and compassionate atmosphere. The pulsating vitality permeates through the students and teachers, creating a vibrant and dynamic learning environment. Today, The institution has indubitably etched its presence, not only in the city but throughout the nation, emerging as one of the foremost new-age schools, commanding admiration and respect.
